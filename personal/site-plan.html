Home page

Importance of Exercise

Exercise is widely recognized as a staple to healthy living. Those who exercise have increased quality of life with clear benefits such as:

  • Strengthened muscle, bone, and other organs
  • Increased mental capacity and self control
  • Weight management
  • Reduced risk of disease
  • Longevity
  • Improved mood and self-confidence
  • Lookin' good

About us

Tough Nut is an organization dedicated to helping others develop healthy exercise routines. We provide a few basic routines to beginners, and offer personal trainer services local to Rexburg, Idaho.

Explore Exercises

Convict Conditioning

Convict Conditioning is an old-school calisthenics routine. It places focus on natural and progressively difficult movements. It targets all muscle groups and encourages slow but steady progress to build the other body tissues and helps get a steady routine.

Convict Conditioning uses the following exercises, with variations based on your current level:

  • Pushups
  • Squats
  • Pullups
  • Leg Raises
  • Bridges
  • Handstand Pushups

Body by Science

Body by Science is a H.I.T. lifting workout- 5 lifts, 1 set each lasting 60-90 seconds with 30 seconds between each set; performed 1x per week. You are in and out of the gym in 12-15 min max. No need to perform any cardio during the week, in fact it is encouraged not to as you need to recover from this workout.

It's perfect for any trainee with a busy schedule that just wants to get stronger. It utilizes the most effective workout science can give us so far. It is a quick workout that drains you quickly and completely, and encourages recovery. A gym with the necessary machines is required.

Body by Science uses the following exercises:

  • Seated Row
  • Chest Press
  • Lat Pull Down
  • Overhead Press
  • Leg Press

Meet The Trainers

Jacob Hofhiens

Jacob grew up as a cross country runner and competitive swimmer. He started working on strength training when he was 19 years old while serving a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day saints. Since then, he has worked hard and developed a habit of exercise that he loves. He has a passion for self-improvement and wants to share with the world those things that have helped him.

Mark Anderson

With years of experience in strength training and a background in collegiate athletics, Mark is passionate about making exercise enjoyable and effective for his clients. His personalized approach focuses on building not just muscles, but sustainable healthy habits. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, Mark is here to support and motivate you on your journey to a fitter, healthier you.

Sarah Hawthorne

Introducing Sarah, your dedicated personal trainer committed to guiding you towards your fitness aspirations. With a wealth of experience in strength training and a background in competitive sports, Sarah is enthusiastic about tailoring workouts to suit your unique needs and preferences. Her holistic approach prioritizes not just physical gains, but also mental well-being, ensuring a sustainable journey to a healthier lifestyle. Whether you're taking your first steps into fitness or aiming to push your limits, Sarah's friendly demeanor and expert guidance will empower you to unlock your full potential and achieve lasting results.
